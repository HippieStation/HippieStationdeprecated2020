04 February 2020

QuarianCommando updated:

  • Medical borgs have been given a defibrillator and surgical processor by default.
  • Borg defib and surgical processor have been removed from the tech web, for obvious reasons.

TheChosenEvilOne updated:

  • Atmospheric reaction priority works correctly now, this means hypernob will stop all reactions and reactions should generally be more consistent between rounds.

Weisyl updated:

  • Made some fixes and adjustments to the new mining station.
  • Added air fans to all of the supply shuttles, so that they no longer depressurize every time blast doors are left open.

steamp0rt updated:

  • Pop requirement for the Badmin Gauntlet reduced to 18.
