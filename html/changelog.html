02 September 2019

YoYoBatty updated:

  • Slimes will now eat faster, and get more nutrition.

steamp0rt updated:

  • ERTs no longer stop the round from ending
  • Shooting certain weapons with a Range F guardian is no longer suicide
  • Non-abductor midround rulesets should actually have a chance of happening now.
  • Added a HUD for dominator placement
  • Added a HUD for guardian range.
  • Adds an ending cinematic after THE SNAP happens
  • Stand arrow can now appear in Summon Magic
  • Golden Apples now apply Absorption and Regeneration!
  • Dynamic mode now respects your antag prefs
  • Shuttle refuel delay is now 30 minutes during thanus

29 August 2019

Fikou updated:

  • Requiem stands now properly roll for potential

steamp0rt updated:

  • Abductors can now be a midround antagonist in dynamic.
  • Changed the range for dynamic midround injection to 10-25 minutes, from 15-35 minutes.
  • If there are already 2 cakes, Let There Be Cake! will teleport the most eaten one to the user's feet.
  • Fixed runtime spam with cluwnes
  • New sound effect for Badmin Gauntlet snap
  • When you put the final stone into the Badmin Gauntlet, there will be a 5 second "full power sequence", before you are able to snap.
  • The Snap now pulls from a list of living player mobs, rather than all player mobs total. Should result in half the station's living population actually being snapped.
  • Got rid of The Garden. It was ugly and served no purpose.
  • New screen overlay for The Snap.
  • the Revengers Hulk should now actually be green.
  • The Revengers (or any ERT, really) can no longer be knocked down by shockwave, and all of them now have Reviver/CNS implants.
  • Fixed display order of badmin stone/gauntlet examine.
  • Fixed infinity cake not containing any chemicals.
  • You can no longer go into blood crawl with the Badmin Stones in your possession, they'll be dropped on the floor.
  • Speaking of blood crawl, if someone is stupid enough to revive thanos with cluwne rise (this HAS happened before), he can now put the stones in the gauntlet without a free hand by clicking on them with HARM intent.
  • The ghost stone no longer acts an inescapable ghost magnet.
  • The ghost stone's spiritual fist does more damage per ghost, and has a higher maximum damage (5 damage per ghost, minimum of 3, maximum of 35).
  • Syndie Stone holders using Super Jump will no longer accidentally destroy their clothes (this was kinda funny but it makes it near-useless)
  • Added titanium to the supply pod drop during wardec.
  • Assistants now have a small chance to get the Bluespace Stone (man of steal)
  • When the SNAP happens, if the Revengers have not already been called, they will be called.
  • Ghost stone cluwnes are colored now. Gold if raised with the badmin gauntlet, purple if raised with the ghost stone.
  • Fixed pocket sand being finnicky.
  • As it turns out, the Badmin Gauntlet can overpower even the most powerful of beings.
